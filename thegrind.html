The Grind offers the best coffee on campus for the best price! Located beside TwelvEighty Bar & Grill, The Grind serves quality crafted beverages along with a unique menu featuring baked goods, hot sandwiches and bagels.

The Grind is a fabulous café, serving premium coffee, tea, baked goods and bagels. The space has lots of great seating surrounded by USB connectivity for device charging, while featuring daily specials. In addition to its line-up of espresso, lattés and cappuccinos – all made with 100% Arabica beans.
