H. G. Thode Library

Open 8am - 12am

Social Close to Transit Printers Food Available Engineering Quiet Basement Many Outlets
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Thode Library holds the University Library's collections in science and engineering and has a wide variety of study spaces to meet your needs. Other amenities in the building include the Reactor Cafe (first floor) and the Thode Makerspace (lower level).

H. G. Thode Library is more than just a collection of books. It is your first stop for access to the scholarship and foundational materials available for the sciences and engineering programs at McMaster. As well, it has a variety of study spaces that will fit your study needs, no matter your program.
