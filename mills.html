Mills Memorial Library

Open 8am - 9pm

Social Group Study Rooms Central Location Rarely Full Nice Atmosphere 3D Printers Good Wifi Quiet Many Outlets Computer Access
★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Mills is home to the Humanities and Social Sciences collections, as well as wide range of special services and resources.

The Learning Commons is located on the 2nd floor. Our largest study space - outfitted with desks, comfortable seating, desktop computers, charging stations and multi-function printers. Located throughout the 1st through 5th floors, quiet study areas are for individual study and quiet conversations. No cell phone use, loud noise, music, or rowdiness.
